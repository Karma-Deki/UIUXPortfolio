About Me

    Diving deep into the realms of design and technology, I'm currently pursuing my studies in AI and Data Science at Gyalpozhing College of Information and Technology. Fueled by a relentless passion for crafting seamless user experiences, I've embarked on various UI/UX endeavors, including the development of a RadianceRealm mobile app, the creation of a Bite&Brew website, and the design of a responsive SportyStyle platform. These projects not only showcase my dedication to creating innovative solutions but also reflect my commitment to merging creativity with functionality. Beyond academia, I am driven by a desire to leave a lasting impact through intuitive design and transformative technology. As the renowned designer Dieter Rams once said, "Good design is as little design as possible," and I strive to embody this ethos in all my endeavors, ensuring simplicity and elegance in every interface I create.

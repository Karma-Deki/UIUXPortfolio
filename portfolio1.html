RadianceRealm: Cosmetics App

Project description

UX Case Study

Duration: 14th Febuary - 25th May

Product Overview

Introducing RadianceRealm: your ultimate cosmetic companion. Our app offers a curated selection of beauty products tailored to your preferences and needs. Whether you're a makeup newbie or a seasoned pro, RadianceRealm helps you discover your perfect look effortlessly.


Problem

Aimed to solve the problem of overwhelming choices in the cosmetic market, making it difficult for users to find products suited to their individual needs and preferences. RadianceRealm simplifies this by offering personalized recommendations and a curated selection, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience.


Goal

To create a user-friendly app that provides personalized cosmetic recommendations, simplifying the shopping experience for beauty enthusiasts. By curating a tailored selection of products, we aimed to help users effortlessly discover items that suit their unique preferences and needs.

Target Audience,Key Challenges & Contraints

Target Audience

  • Makeup Enthusiasts: ndividuals passionate about cosmetics, seeking a wide range of beauty products and trends.
  • Makeup Beginners: Beginners interested in exploring makeup options, requiring guidance and tutorials to enhance their skills.
  • Eco-Friendly Shoppers: Users prioritizing sustainable and cruelty-free beauty options, seeking brands aligned with their values.

Key Challenges & Contraints

  • Intense competition: SportyStyle faces fierce competition from other sportswear brands and e-commerce platforms, requiring strategic differentiation to stand out.
  • Technical limitations: Addressing technical challenges such as improving website speed, ensuring the website can handle increased traffic, and optimizing it for mobile devices.
  • Customer service: Providing excellent customer service is essential for addressing inquiries, resolving issues, and maintaining customer satisfaction levels.

Usability study: findings

In my usability studies for the RadianceRealm website, I conducted two rounds of research to gather valuable insights from users. Round 1 revealed challenges with product discovery, checkout process, and community engagement, leading to iterative improvements implemented in Round 2. The findings from these studies informed targeted enhancements to optimize user experience and usability.

Round 1 findings

  • users reported difficulties in discovering new cosmetic products due to cluttered product listings and unclear categorization, highlighting the need for a more intuitive and organized browsing experience.

  • Users want detailed product info like ingredients and usage tips, indicating a need to enhance product pages for better user trust.

Round 2 findings

  • significant improvements in onboarding, with users reporting smoother account creation and clearer guidance through the initial setup process.

  • Navigation enhancements, such as a redesigned menu structure and clearer labeling, improved user satisfaction and ease of use.

  • higher satisfaction with the revised navigation, finding it easier to explore different sections of the website and locate desired products efficiently.

Overview

Bite&Brew Website

Project description

UX Case Study

Duration: 14th Febuary - 25th May

Product Overview

Introducing the Bite&Brew website: your go-to for seamless ordering from your favorite spot. Tailored for busy professionals and foodies, it ensures quality meals on the go.


Problem

To address the hassle of long wait times and busy schedules, offering a convenient solution for ordering meals efficiently.


Goal

To streamline the ordering process for busy individuals while maintaining the high quality of our restaurant's offerings.

Target Audience,Key Challenges & Contraints

Target Audience

  • Busy professionals: Professionals with hectic schedules seeking convenient dining solutions that fit their busy lifestyles.
  • Foodies: Enthusiasts who appreciate quality cuisine and seek diverse culinary experiences delivered to their doorstep.

Key Challenges & Contraints

  • Technological Integration: Integrating diverse restaurant menus and ordering systems into a seamless platform may pose technical challenges.
  • User Experience: Ensuring a user-friendly interface and intuitive ordering process for both novice and experienced users is crucial.
  • Quality Control: Maintaining food quality during delivery and ensuring customer satisfaction requires rigorous quality control measures.

Roles and Responsibilities

My Role

UI/UX Designer: Responsible for the overall design and user experience for Bite&Brew Wbsite.

responsibility

My responsibility involves conducting user research, designing wireframes, developing the website's features, testing and iterating, and implementing the final design and functionality.

User research: summary

we conducted surveys and interviews with a diverse range of individuals, including busy professionals and avid food enthusiasts. Initially, we assumed that convenience would be the primary factor driving their dining decisions. However, through our research, we discovered that while convenience was indeed important, users also highly valued the quality and freshness of their meals. This insight led us to prioritize both convenience and quality in the design of the Bite&Brew app, ensuring that users could enjoy a seamless ordering experience without compromising on the deliciousness of their food.

User Pain Points

1

Inefficient Ordering Experience:

Designing a user-friendly interface prioritizing convenience and quality, focusing on streamlining the ordering process while emphasizing freshness and excellence in every dish.

2

Delivery Time and Estimates:

Lengthy delivery times and unreliable estimates frustrate users, impacting their overall experience.To improve user experience, I will focus on accurate delivery estimates and faster delivery times in our designs.

3

Order Tracking and Real-time Updates:

To enhance user confidence, I'll focus on implementing real-time order tracking and timely updates in our designs.

Usability study

Usability Study:Parameters

  • Navigation Ease: Assess how easily users can move through the website.
  • Information Clarity: Check the understandability and accessibility of menu items and descriptions.
  • User Feedback: Gather user opinions on overall experience and satisfaction.

Usability Study:Findings

  • Navigation Ease: Users found the website easy to navigate, with clear menus and categories.
  • Information Clarity: Menu items and descriptions were clear, but users recommended including more detailed ingredient lists.
  • User Feedback: Overall user satisfaction was high, with positive comments about the intuitive design and ease of use.

    • Takeaways

    Impact

  • The redesigned Bite&Brew website has significantly improved user engagement and satisfaction. A participant noted, "Ordering my favorite meals has never been easier. The website is intuitive and accessible, making the whole process seamless."

    • What I learned

  • Through the Bite&Brew project, I learned the importance of prioritizing accessibility and user-centric design. Conducting usability tests and incorporating user feedback highlighted the value of iterative improvements. This project also underscored the necessity of ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience for all users, regardless of their abilities or devices.
